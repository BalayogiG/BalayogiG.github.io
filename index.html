About Me

I am a Project Associate at the Centre for Responsible AI (CeRAI), Indian Institute of Technology Madras, where I contribute to the Conversational AI Evaluation Tool Project. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Pondicherry University, where I specialized in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Usable Security. My research focuses on improving the usability and security of digital systems, with particular attention to the needs of persons with visual impairments.

My current research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Algorithm Design, with a focus on their applications in real-world systems. I am particularly interested in enhancing the efficiency and interpretability of neural networks, and in developing scalable, explainable AI solutions.

I have published my research in international conferences and journals, contributing to advancements in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), usable security, and the practical applications of cyber security in real-world systems.
